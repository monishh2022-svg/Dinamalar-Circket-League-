About The League

The Premier Corporate Cricket Experience

The Dinamalar Premier Cricket League brings together 32 corporate teams for an electrifying leather ball tournament. From the inaugural knockout round through the Grand Final, every match is a battle for corporate glory.

Backed by Dinamalar — Tamil Nadu's most-read newspaper — the DPL offers sponsors unparalleled access to Chennai's corporate decision-makers through print, digital, and on-ground activations.

The Title Sponsor slot has been secured by Namma Family Group. The Powered By and Ball/Umpire sponsorship opportunities remain open for the right partners.
